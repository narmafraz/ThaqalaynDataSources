(It has been narrated) from Abu Abdullahasws, said, ‘I asked himasws about the instigation between the animals, so heasws said: ‘All of it is abhorred, except for the dog’.[2649]

عَنْهُ عَنْ عَلِيِّ بْنِ الْحَكَمِ عَنْ أَبَانٍ عَنْ مِسْمَعٍ قَالَ سَأَلْتُ أَبَا عَبْدِ اللَّهِ ( عليه السلام ) عَنِ التَّحْرِيشِ بَيْنَ الْبَهَائِمِ فَقَالَ أَكْرَهُ ذَلِكَ إِلَّا الْكِلَابَ .

From him, from Ali Bin Al Hakam, from Aban, from Misma’a who said,

‘I asked Abu Abdullahasws about the instigation between the animals. So heasws said: ‘Iasws dislike that, except for the dogs’.[2650]
