H 14768 – From him, from Al-Moala, from Al-Hassan, from Aban, from Abu hashim who said:

When they came out with Aliasws, Fatimaasws also came out having placed the shirt of the Rasool Allahsaww upon herasws head, taking herasws two sonsasws by the hand. So sheasws said: ‘What is it between measws and you, O Abu Bakr that you want to orphan myasws two sonsasws and widow measws from myasws husbandasws? By Allahazwj! Had it not been a sin, Iasws would have released myasws hair and cried out to myasws Lordazwj!’ So a man from the people said, ‘What do you want (from all) this?’ Then sheasws took himasws (Aliasws) by hisasws hand and left with himasws’.
