H 14932 – Muhammad Bin Yahya, from Ahmad Bin Muhammad, from Ibn Fazzaal, from Abu Jameela, from Muhammad Bin Ali Al-Halby who said:

I heard Abu Abdullahasws saying: ‘The differing of the Clan of Abbas is from the inevitable, and the Call is from the inevitable, and the coming out of Al-Qaimasws is from the inevitable’. I said, ‘And How would be the Call?’ Heasws said: ‘A Caller will Call out from the sky at the beginning of the day: ‘Indeed! Aliasws and hisasws Shites, they are the winners’. Heasws said: ‘And a Caller will Call out at the end of the day: ‘Indeed! Usman and his Shites, they are the winners’.
