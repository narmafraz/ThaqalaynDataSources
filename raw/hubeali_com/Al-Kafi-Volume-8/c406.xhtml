H 14809 – Ali, from his father, from Ibn Abu Umeyr, from Muhammad Bin Abu Hamza and someone else, has reported the following:

Abu Abdullahasws says that the Rasool Allahsaww said: ‘There is goodness for you in mysaww being alive and goodness for you in mysaww passing away’. So they said, ‘O Rasool Allahsaww! As for yoursaww being alive, so we have known that. So what is (goodness) for us in yoursaww passing away?’ Hesaww said: ‘As for mysaww being alive, so Allahazwj Mighty and Majestic has Said: “[8:33] But Allah was not going to chastise them while you were among them”. And as for mysaww passing away, your deeds would get presented to mesaww, so Isaww would be seeking Forgiveness for you’.

14810- عَلِيُّ بْنُ إِبْرَاهِيمَ عَنْ أَبِيهِ عَنِ ابْنِ أَبِي عُمَيْرٍعَنْ هِشَامِ بْنِ سَالِمٍ قَالَ قَالَ أَبُو عَبْدِ اللَّهِ (عليه السلام) إِنَّ مِمَّنْ يَنْتَحِلُ هَذَا الْأَمْرَ لَيَكْذِبُ حَتَّى إِنَّ الشَّيْطَانَ لَيَحْتَاجُ إِلَى كَذِبِهِ.
