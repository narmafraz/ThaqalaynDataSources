H 14468 – And from him, from Muhammad Bin Ali, from Ibn Muskan, from Muyassar has narrated:

It was said to Abu Ja’farasws about the Statement of Allahazwj Mighty and Majestic: “[7:56] Do no mischief on the earth, after it hath been set in order”, so heasws said: ‘O Muyassar, verily the earth used to be in a state of disorder, so Allahazwj Mighty and Majestic Corrected it by Hisazwj Prophetsaww, therefore Heazwj Said:“[7:56] Do no mischief on the earth, after it hath been set in order”.
