H 14461 – Sahl, from Muhammad, from his father has narrated:

Abu Abdullahasws having said when it was said to himasws “[88:1] Has not there come to you the news of the overwhelming calamity?”, heasws said: ‘They will be overwhelmed by the sword of the Rising One (Al-Qaimasws)’. He said, ‘I said, (What about) “[88:2] Some faces, that Day, will be humiliated”?’ Heasws said: ‘They will be so humiliated that they will not be able to bear the exclusion’. He said, ‘I asked. ‘(What about) “[88:3] Labouring”?’ Heasws said: ‘Labouring for other than what Allahazwj Revealed’. I then asked, (What about) “[88:3] Appointing”?’ Heasws replied: ‘Having appointed other than the Masterasws of the Command (Wali Al-Amr)’. He says then I asked, (What about) “[88:4] Entering into burning fire”?’ Heasws replied: ‘Entering into the fire of the war in the world during the era of the Rising One (Al-Qaimasws), and in the Hereafter the Fire of Hell’.
