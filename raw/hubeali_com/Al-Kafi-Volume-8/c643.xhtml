H 15037 – Ali Bin Ibrahim, from his father, from Ibn Abu Umeyr, from Suleym Al-Fara’a, from the one whom he mentioned:

‘Abu Abdullahasws, said in hisasws Hadeeth that: ‘When the Angel said: ‘Its Lordazwj Made me enter it’, Ibrahimas recognised that he is the Angel of Death. So heas said to him: ‘What made you descend?’ He said; ‘I have come to give good news to a man whom Allahazwj Blessed and High has Taken as a friend’. So Ibrahimas said to him: ‘So who is this man?’ The Angel said to him: ‘And what do youas want from himas?’ So Ibrahimas said to him: ‘Ias will serve himas for the days of myas life’. The Angel said to himas: ‘Youas are himas’.
