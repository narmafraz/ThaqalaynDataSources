H 14908 – Humeyd Bin Ziyad, from Muhammad Bin Ayyub, from Muhammad Bin Ziyad, from Asbaat Bin Saalim, who has narrated the following:

Abu Abdullahasws having said: ‘When Aamina Bint Wahabas went behind the curtain, and was taken before the ‘nazool’ of the Prophetsaww, Fatima Bin Asadas the wife of Abu Talibas was present with heras. Sheas did not cease to be with heras until shesa was blessed. So one of themas said to the otheras, ‘Did youas see what Ias saw?’ Sheas said, ‘And what did youas see?’ Sheas said, ‘This light which has brightened up what is in between the East and the West and what is in between the two as well’. Then Abu Talibas came up to themas and said to themas both, ‘What is the matter with youas two? Which thing has astounded youas two?’ So Fatimaas informed himas of the light which sheas had seen. So Abu Talibas said to heras, ‘Indeed! Ias give youas good news!’ Sheas said, ‘Yes’. So heas said, ‘Youas will be blessed with the oneasws who will be the successorasws of this newbornsaww’.

14909- مُحَمَّدُ بْنُ أَحْمَدَ عَنْ عَبْدِ اللَّهِ بْنِ الصَّلْتِ عَنْ يُونُسَ وَ عَنْ عَبْدِ الْعَزِيزِ بْنِ الْمُهْتَدِي عَنْ رَجُلٍ عَنْ أَبِي الْحَسَنِ الْمَاضِي (عليه السلام) فِي قَوْلِهِ تَعَالَى مَنْ ذَا الَّذِي يُقْرِضُ اللَّهَ قَرْضاً حَسَناً فَيُضاعِفَهُ لَهُ وَ لَهُ أَجْرٌ كَرِيمٌ قَالَ صِلَةُ الْإِمَامِ فِي دَوْلَةِ الْفَسَقَةِ.
