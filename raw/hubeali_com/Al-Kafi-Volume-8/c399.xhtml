H 14802 – Yahya Bin Umraan, from Haroun Bin Kharjat, from Abu Baseer, who has narrated the following:

Abu Abdullahasws regarding the Statement of Allahazwj Mighty and Majestic: “[21:84] and We gave him his household (that he had lost) and the like thereof along with them”. I said, ‘His (Prophet Ayubas) children, how was heas given the like of them, with them?’ Heasws said: ‘Heazwj Revived for himas from hisas children who had died before that of natural causes, and the like (number) of those who had died on that day’.

14803- يَحْيَى الْحَلَبِيُّ عَنِ الْمُثَنَّى عَنْ أَبِي بَصِيرٍ عَنْ أَبِي عَبْدِ اللَّهِ (عليه السلام) فِي قَوْلِ اللَّهِ عَزَّ وَ جَلَّ كَأَنَّما أُغْشِيَتْ وُجُوهُهُمْ قِطَعاً مِنَ اللَّيْلِ مُظْلِماً قَالَ أَ مَا تَرَى الْبَيْتَ إِذَا كَانَ اللَّيْلُ كَانَ أَشَدَّ سَوَاداً مِنْ خَارِجٍ فَلِذَلِكَ هُمْ يَزْدَادُونَ سَوَاداً.
