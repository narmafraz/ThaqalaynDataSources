H 14920 – Ali Bin Ibrahim, from his father, from ibn Abu Umeyr, from Ibrahim the owner of the Barley, from Kaseer Bin Kalsama, who has narrated the following:

Imamasws, said, regarding the Statement of Allahazwj Mighty and Majestic: “[2:37] Then Adam received (some) words from his Lord”, heasws said: ‘There is no god except Youazwj, Glory be to Youazwj our Allahazwj and by Yourazwj Praise, I have acted in an evil manner and have been unjust to myself, so Forgive me and Youazwj are the best of the Forgivers. There is no god except Youazwj. Glory be to Youazwj our Allahazwj, and by Yourazwj Praise. I have acted in an evil manner and have been unjust to myself, so Forgive me and be Merciful to me, and Youazwj are the Most Merciful of the merciful ones. There is no god except Youazwj. Glory be to Youazwj our Allahazwj, and by Yourazwj Praise. I have acted in an evil manner and have been unjust to myself, so Turn (with Forgiveness) to me for Youazwj are the Merciful Forgiver’.

وَ فِي رِوَايَةٍ أُخْرَى فِي قَوْلِهِ عَزَّ وَ جَلَّ فَتَلَقَّى آدَمُ مِنْ رَبِّهِ كَلِماتٍ قَالَ سَأَلَهُ بِحَقِّ مُحَمَّدٍ وَ عَلِيٍّ وَ الْحَسَنِ وَ الْحُسَيْنِ وَ فَاطِمَةَ صَلَّى اللَّهُ عَلَيْهِمْ.

And in another report regarding the Statement of the Mighty and Majestic: “[2:37] Then Adam received (some) words from his Lord”, heasws said: ‘Heas asked for (forgiveness for) the sake of Muhammadsaww, and Aliasws, and Al-Hassanasws, and Al-Husaynasws, and Fatimaasws’.

14921- مُحَمَّدُ بْنُ يَحْيَى عَنْ أَحْمَدَ بْنِ مُحَمَّدِ بْنِ عِيسَى وَ عَلِيُّ بْنُ إِبْرَاهِيمَ عَنْ أَبِيهِ عَنِ ابْنِ أَبِي عُمَيْرٍ عَنْ أَبِي أَيُّوبَ الْخَزَّازِ عَنْ أَبِي بَصِيرٍ عَنْ أَبِي عَبْدِ اللَّهِ (عليه السلام) قَالَ لَمَّا رَأَى إِبْرَاهِيمُ (عليه السلام) مَلَكُوتَ السَّمَاوَاتِ وَ الْأَرْضِ الْتَفَتَ فَرَأَى رَجُلًا يَزْنِي فَدَعَا عَلَيْهِ فَمَاتَ ثُمَّ رَأَى آخَرَ فَدَعَا عَلَيْهِ فَمَاتَ حَتَّى رَأَى ثَلَاثَةً فَدَعَا عَلَيْهِمْ فَمَاتُوا
