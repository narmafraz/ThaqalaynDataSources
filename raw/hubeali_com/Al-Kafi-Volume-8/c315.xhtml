H 14721 – Ali Bin Ibrahim, from his father, from Al-nawfaly, from Al-Sakuny, who has narrated the following:

Abu Abdullahasws has said that the Rasool Allahsaww said: ‘There is nothing more intense than the grief of a woman, nor anything more remote than the separation of the death, and more intense than all of that is poverty. One flatters his companion, who then does not give him anything’.
