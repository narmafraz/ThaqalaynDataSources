H 14839 – Muhammad Bin Yahya, from Ahmad Bin Muhammad Bin Isa, from Ali Bin Muhammad Bin Asheym, from Safwaan Bin Yahya who said:

I asked Abu Al-Hassan Al-Rezaasws about Zi Al-Fiqaar (Zulfiqaar), the sword of the Rasool Allahsaww. Heasws said: ‘Jibraeel as came down with it from the sky, and it hilt was made of silver’.

حَدِيثُ نُوحٍ ( عليه السلام ) يَوْمَ الْقِيَامَةِ
