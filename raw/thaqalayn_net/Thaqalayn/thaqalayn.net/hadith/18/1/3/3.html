3. The (group of) fives - Hadith #3

Back to chapter

عنه، عن محمد بن علي، عن عبد الرحمن بن محمد الاسدي، عن حريب الغزال، عن صدقة القتاب، عن الحسن البصري، قال كنت مع أبى جعفر (ع) بمنى وقد مات رجل من قريش فقال: يا با سعيد قم بنا إلى جنازته فلما دخلنا المقابر قال: ألا أخبركم بخمس خصال هي من البر والبريد عوإلى الجنة؟ - قلت: بلى، قال: إخفاء المصيبة وكتمانها، والصدقة تعطيها بيمينك لا تعلم بها شمالك، وبر الوالدين فان برهما لله رضى، والاكثار من قول: " لا حول ولا قوة إلا بالله العلى العظيم " فانه من كنوز الجنة، والحب لمحمد وآل محمد (صلى الله عليه وعليهم أجمعين).


3. From him, from Muhammad Bin Ali, from Abdul Rahman Bin Muhammad Al Asady, from Hareyb Al Gazaal, from Sadaqa Al Qataab, from Al Hassan Al Basry who said, ‘I was with Abu Ja’far (a.s.) at Mina, and a man from Quraish had died. So he (a.s.) said: ‘Shall I (a.s.) inform you of five qualities which are from righteousness, and the message to the Paradise?’ I said, ‘Yes’. He (a.s.) said: ‘Hide your difficulties and conceal them; and the charity, give it with your right hand such that your left hand does not know about it; and the righteousness with the parents, for in being good to them is Allah (azwj)’s Pleasure; and frequent in saying, ‘There is no Power nor Strength except with Allah (azwj), the Exalted, the Magnificent’, for it is from the treasures of the Paradise; and the love for Muhammad (saww) and the Progeny (a.s.) of Muhammad (saww)’.


Grading:

The gradings provided are observations of the chain of narrators, a hadith with a weak or unknown chain may still be correct, and a hadith with a reliable chain may still be incorrect.

Gradings may also differ depending on the methodology of the scholar, for example Allamah Baqir al-Majlisi's gradings would be more lenient compared to Shaykh Asif al-Mohseni's.
