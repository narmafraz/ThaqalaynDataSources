11. The precaution in the Religion and the acting by the Sunnah - Hadith #17

Back to chapter

عنه، عن بعض أصحابنا، عن عبد الله بن عبد الرحمن البصري، عن ابن مسكان عن أبي عبد الله عليه السلام، عن أبيه، عن علي بن الحسين عليهم السلام قال: مر موسى بن عمران عليه السلام برجل وهو رافع يده إلى السماء يدعو الله فانطلق موسى في حاجته فبات سبعة أيام ثم رجع إليه وهو رافع يده إلى السماء فقال: يا رب هذا عبدك رافع يديه إليك يسألك حاجته ويسألك المغفرة منذ سبعة أيام لا تستجيب له! قال: فأوحى الله إليه يا موسى لو دعاني حتى يسقط يداه أو ينقطع لسانه ما استجبت له حتى يأتيني من الباب الذي أمرته


17. From him, from one of our companions, from Abdullah Bin Abdul Rahman Al nasry, from Ibn Muskaan, (It has been narrated) from Abu Abdullah (a.s.), from his father, from Ali (a.s.) Bin Al-Husayn (a.s.) having said: ‘Musa (as) Bin Imran (as) passed by a man, and he had raised his hands towards the sky supplicating to Allah (azwj). So Musa (as) went away for his (as) needs, then returned to him after seven days, and he had his hands raised towards the sky. So he (as) said: ‘O Lord (azwj)! This is Your (azwj) servant, raising his hands to You (azwj), Asking You (azwj) for a need, and asks You (azwj) for Forgiveness since the last seven days, You (azwj) has not Answered him?’ The Imam (a.s.) said: ‘So Allah (azwj) Revealed unto him (as): “O Musa (as)! Even if he were to supplicate to Me (azwj) until his hands fall down, and his tongue is cut-off, I (azwj) will not Answer him until he comes to Me (azwj) from the Door which I (azwj) have Commanded him by (My (azwj) Prophet (as)/Imam (a.s.))”.


Grading:

The gradings provided are observations of the chain of narrators, a hadith with a weak or unknown chain may still be correct, and a hadith with a reliable chain may still be incorrect.

Gradings may also differ depending on the methodology of the scholar, for example Allamah Baqir al-Majlisi's gradings would be more lenient compared to Shaykh Asif al-Mohseni's.
