44. Beginning of the creation - Hadith #1

Back to chapter

عنه، عن أبيه، عن فضاله بن أيوب، عن جميل بن دراج، عن زرارة، عن أبي عبد الله (ع) في قول الله تعالى: " وإذا أخذ ربك من بني آدم من ظهورهم ذريتهم و أشهدهم على أنفسهم " قال: كان ذلك معاينة لله، فأنساهم المعاينة وأثبت الاقرار في صدورهم، ولو لا ذلك ما عرف أحد خالقه ولا رازقه، وهو قول الله: " ولئن سئلتهم من خلقهم ليقولن الله ".


1. From him, from his father, from Fazala Bin Ayoub, from Jameel Bin Daraaj, from Zarara, (It has been narrated) from Abu Abdullah (a.s.) regarding the Words of Allah (azwj) the High [7:172] And when your Lord brought forth from the Children of Adam, from their backs, their descendants, and made them testify against their own souls. The Imam (a.s.) said: ‘That was a Preview of Allah (azwj), so they forgot the preview, and the acceptance was affirmed in their chests. And had it not been for that, no one would have recognised who his Creator was, or who his Sustainer was. And these are the Words of Allah (azwj) [43:87] And if you should ask them who created them, they would certainly say: Allah’.


Grading:

The gradings provided are observations of the chain of narrators, a hadith with a weak or unknown chain may still be correct, and a hadith with a reliable chain may still be incorrect.

Gradings may also differ depending on the methodology of the scholar, for example Allamah Baqir al-Majlisi's gradings would be more lenient compared to Shaykh Asif al-Mohseni's.
