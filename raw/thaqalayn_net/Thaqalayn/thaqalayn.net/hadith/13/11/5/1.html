5. Fatimah (SA) after the khilafat of Abu Bakr - Hadith #1

Back to chapter

5 - قال: أخبرني أبو الحسن علي بن محمد الكاتب قال: أخبرني الحسن بن علي الزعفراني قال: أخبرني إبراهيم بن محمد الثقفي قال: أخبرنا أبو إسماعيل العطار قال: أخبرنا ابن لهيعة، عن أبي الأسود ، عن عروة بن الزبير قال: لما بايع الناس أبا بكر خرجت فاطمة بنت محمد صلى الله عليه وآله وسلم فوقفت على بابها وقالت: ما رأيت كاليوم قط، حضروا أسوء محضر، تركوا نبيهم صلى الله عليه وآله جنازة بين أظهرنا واستبدوا بالأمر دوننا.


5. He said: Abul Hasan ‘Ali ibn Muhammad al-Katib reported to me from al-Hasan ibn ‘Ali al-Zafarani, who reported from Ibrahim ibn Muhammad al-Thaqafi, who reported from Abu Ismail al-Attar, from Ibn Luhaya’h, from Abul Aswad, from Urwah ibn al-Zubayr that: When people swore allegiance to Abu Bakr, Fatimah, the daughter of (Prophet) Muhammad, peace be upon him and his progeny, came to the door of her house and said: "Never have I witnessed a day like this. They have indeed, set a worst record. They left their Prophet, peace be upon him and his progeny, with his corpse in our midst and preoccupied themselves with the matter (of succession) without us."


Grading:

The gradings provided are observations of the chain of narrators, a hadith with a weak or unknown chain may still be correct, and a hadith with a reliable chain may still be incorrect.

Gradings may also differ depending on the methodology of the scholar, for example Allamah Baqir al-Majlisi's gradings would be more lenient compared to Shaykh Asif al-Mohseni's.
