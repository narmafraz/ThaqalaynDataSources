45. Description of Wudu before Eating Food - Hadith #3

Back to chapter

عَلِيُّ بْنُ مُحَمَّدٍ عَنْ أَحْمَدَ بْنِ مُحَمَّدٍ عَنِ الْفَضْلِ بْنِ الْمُبَارَكِ عَنِ الْفَضْلِ بْنِ يُونُسَ قَالَ لَمَّا تَغَدَّى عِنْدِي أَبُو الْحَسَنِ ( عليه السلام ) وَ جِي‏ءَ بِالطَّسْتِ بُدِئَ بِهِ ( عليه السلام ) وَ كَانَ فِي صَدْرِ الْمَجْلِسِ فَقَالَ ( عليه السلام ) ابْدَأْ بِمَنْ عَلَى يَمِينِكَ فَلَمَّا تَوَضَّأَ وَاحِدٌ أَرَادَ الْغُلَامُ أَنْ يَرْفَعَ الطَّسْتَ فَقَالَ لَهُ أَبُو الْحَسَنِ ( عليه السلام ) دَعْهَا وَ اغْسِلُوا أَيْدِيَكُمْ فِيهَا .


3. Ali ibn Muhammad has narrated from Ahmad ibn Muhammad from al-Fadl ibn al-Mubarak from al-Fadl ibn Yunus who has said the following: “When abu al-Hassan, ‘Alayhi al-Salam, had lunch and the hand-basin was brought, he (the Imam) was the first to wash his hand while he (the Imam) was sitting in the middle of the assembly room, and he (the Imam) said, ‘Begin with one who is on your right side . 5 When one made wudu the servant wanted to remove the hand-basin, but abu al-Hassan, ‘Alayhi al-Salam, said to him, ‘Leave it so that you can wash your hands in it . 555


Grading:

Allamah Baqir al-Majlisi: مجهول - Mir‘at al ‘Uqul Fi Sharh Akhbar Al al Rasul (22/104)

The gradings provided are observations of the chain of narrators, a hadith with a weak or unknown chain may still be correct, and a hadith with a reliable chain may still be incorrect.

Gradings may also differ depending on the methodology of the scholar, for example Allamah Baqir al-Majlisi's gradings would be more lenient compared to Shaykh Asif al-Mohseni's.
