180. Lawful Matters for a Husband during His Wife’s Menses - Hadith #4

Back to chapter

4- مُحَمَّدُ بْنُ يَحْيَى عَنْ سَلَمَةَ بْنِ الْخَطَّابِ عَنْ عَلِيِّ بْنِ الْحَسَنِ عَنْ مُحَمَّدِ بْنِ زِيَادٍ عَنْ أَبَانِ بْنِ عُثْمَانَ وَالْحُسَيْنِ بْنِ أَبِي يُوسُفَ عَنْ عَبْدِ الْمَلِكِ بْنِ عَمْرٍو قَالَ سَأَلْتُ أَبَا عَبْدِ اللهِ (عَلَيْهِ السَّلام) مَا يَحِلُّ لِلرَّجُلِ مِنَ الْمَرْأَةِ وَهِيَ حَائِضٌ قَالَ كُلُّ شَيْ‏ءٍ غَيْرَ الْفَرْجِ قَالَ ثُمَّ قَالَ إِنَّمَا الْمَرْأَةُ لُعْبَةُ الرَّجُلِ.


4. Muhammad ibn Yahya has narrated from Salmah ibn a-Khattab from Ali ibn al-Husayn from Muhammad ibn Ziyad from Aban ibn ‘Uthman and al-Husayn ibn Yusuf from ‘Abd al-Malik ibn ‘Amr who has said the following: “I once asked abu ‘Abd Allah, ‘Alayhi al-Salam, about what is permissible for a man to enjoy with his wife during her experiencing Hayd (menses). He (the Imam) said, ‘Everything except her vulva.’ He (the Imam) then said, ‘A Woman is just like a doll for man.’”


Grading:

Allamah Baqir al-Majlisi: ضعيف - Mir‘at al ‘Uqul Fi Sharh Akhbar Al al Rasul (20/382)

The gradings provided are observations of the chain of narrators, a hadith with a weak or unknown chain may still be correct, and a hadith with a reliable chain may still be incorrect.

Gradings may also differ depending on the methodology of the scholar, for example Allamah Baqir al-Majlisi's gradings would be more lenient compared to Shaykh Asif al-Mohseni's.
