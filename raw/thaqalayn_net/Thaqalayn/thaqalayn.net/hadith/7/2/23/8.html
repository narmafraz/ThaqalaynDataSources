23. Son of a Brother with a Grandfather - Hadith #8

Back to chapter

8- مُحَمَّدُ بْنُ يَحْيَى عَنْ أَحْمَدَ بْنِ مُحَمَّدٍ وَعِدَّةٌ مِنْ أَصْحَابِنَا عَنْ سَهْلِ بْنِ زِيَادٍ جَمِيعاً عَنِ ابْنِ مَحْبُوبٍ عَنِ الْحَسَنِ بْنِ صَالِحٍ قَالَ سَأَلْتُ أَبَا عَبْدِ اللهِ (عَلَيْهِ السَّلام) عَنِ امْرَأَةٍ مُمْلَكَةٍ لَمْ يَدْخُلْ بِهَا زَوْجُهَا مَاتَتْ وَتَرَكَتْ أُمَّهَا وَأَخَوَيْنِ لَهَا مِنْ أَبِيهَا وَأُمِّهَا وَجَدَّهَا أَبَا أُمِّهَا وَزَوْجَهَا قَالَ يُعْطَى الزَّوْجُ النِّصْفَ وَتُعْطَى الأمُّ الْبَاقِيَ وَلا يُعْطَى الْجَدُّ شَيْئاً لأنَّ ابْنَتَهُ حَجَبَتْهُ عَنِ الْمِيرَاثِ وَلا يُعْطَى الإخْوَةُ شَيْئاً.


8. Muhammad ibn Yahya has narrated from Ahmad ibn Muhammad and a number of our people have narrated from Sahl ibn Ziyad all from ibn Mahbub from al-Hassan ibn Salih who has narrated the following: “I once asked abu ‘Abd Allah, ‘Alayhi al-Salam, about the case of a woman who is married, but before going to bed with her husband, has died leaving behind her mother, two brothers from her father and mother and her grandfather, who is the father of her mother, and her husband. He (the Imam) said, ‘The husband receives one-half and the rest belongs to her mother. The grandfather does not receive anything; his daughter bars him from inheritance and brothers do not receive anything.’”


Grading:

Allamah Baqir al-Majlisi: ضعيف على المشهور - Mir‘at al ‘Uqul Fi Sharh Akhbar Al al Rasul (3/169)

The gradings provided are observations of the chain of narrators, a hadith with a weak or unknown chain may still be correct, and a hadith with a reliable chain may still be incorrect.

Gradings may also differ depending on the methodology of the scholar, for example Allamah Baqir al-Majlisi's gradings would be more lenient compared to Shaykh Asif al-Mohseni's.
