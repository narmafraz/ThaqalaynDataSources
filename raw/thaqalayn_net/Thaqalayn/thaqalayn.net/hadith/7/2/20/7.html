20. Inheritance of Brothers and Sisters with Children - Hadith #7

Back to chapter

مُحَمَّدُ بْنُ إِسْمَاعِيلَ عَنِ الْفَضْلِ بْنِ شَاذَانَ عَنِ ابْنِ أَبِي عُمَيْرٍ عَنْ جَمِيلِ بْنِ دَرَّاجٍ عَنْ بُكَيْرٍ عَنْ أَبِي جَعْفَرٍ (عَلَيْهِ السَّلام) قَالَ سَأَلَهُ رَجُلٌ عَنْ أُخْتَيْنِ وَزَوْجٍ فَقَالَ النِّصْفُ وَالنِّصْفُ فَقَالَ الرَّجُلُ أَصْلَحَكَ اللهُ قَدْ سَمَّى اللهُ لَهُمَا أَكْثَرَ مِنْ هَذَا لَهُمَا الثُّلُثَانِ فَقَالَ مَا تَقُولُ فِي أَخٍ وَزَوْجٍ فَقَالَ النِّصْفُ وَالنِّصْفُ فَقَالَ أَلَيْسَ قَدْ سَمَّى اللهُ الْمَالَ فَقَالَ وَهُوَ يَرِثُها إِنْ لَمْ يَكُنْ لَها وَلَدٌ.


7. Muhammad ibn ’Isma‘il has narrated from al-Fadl ibn Shadhan from ibn abu ‘Umayr from Jamil ibn Darraj from Bukayr who has narrated the following: “A man once asked abu Ja‘far, ‘Alayhi al-Salam, about two sisters and a husband. He (the Imam) said, ‘It is half and half.’ The man then said, ‘I pray to Allah to keep you well, Allah has mentioned their share to be more than that. Their share is two-thirds.’ He then asked, ‘What do you say about a brother and husband?’ He (the Imam) said, ‘It is half and half.’ He asked, ‘Has Allah not given the entire legacy, saying, “. . . and he inherits her if she has no children.”?”’


Grading:

Allamah Baqir al-Majlisi: مجهول كالحسن - Mir‘at al ‘Uqul Fi Sharh Akhbar Al al Rasul (3/153)

The gradings provided are observations of the chain of narrators, a hadith with a weak or unknown chain may still be correct, and a hadith with a reliable chain may still be incorrect.

Gradings may also differ depending on the methodology of the scholar, for example Allamah Baqir al-Majlisi's gradings would be more lenient compared to Shaykh Asif al-Mohseni's.
