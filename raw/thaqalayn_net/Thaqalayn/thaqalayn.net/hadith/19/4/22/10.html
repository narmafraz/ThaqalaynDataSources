22. The Endive - Hadith #10

Back to chapter

عنه، عن أبيه، عن أحمد بن سليمان، عن أبى بصير، قال: سأل رجل أبا - عبد الله (ع) عن البقل وأنا عنده، فقال: الهندبا لنا. وقال الرضا (ع): عليكم بأكل بقلة الهندبا فانه تزيد في المال والولد، ومن أحب أن يكثر ماله وولده فليدمن أكل الهندبا.


10. From him, from his father, from Ahmad Bin Suleyman, from Abu Baseer who said, ‘A man asked Abu Abdullah (a.s.) about the vegetables and I was in his (a.s.) presence, so he (a.s.) said: ‘The endive is for us (a.s.)’. And Al-Ridha (a.s.) said: ‘It is upon you with the eating of the endive, for it increases in the wealth and the births. And the one who would love to have his wealth and his births to be abundant, so he should be habitual in eating the endive’.


Grading:

The gradings provided are observations of the chain of narrators, a hadith with a weak or unknown chain may still be correct, and a hadith with a reliable chain may still be incorrect.

Gradings may also differ depending on the methodology of the scholar, for example Allamah Baqir al-Majlisi's gradings would be more lenient compared to Shaykh Asif al-Mohseni's.
