22. The Endive - Hadith #6

Back to chapter

عنه عن على بن الحكم، عن مثنى بن زياد، عن أبى عبد الله (ع) قال: قال أمير المؤمنين (ع): كلوا الهندبا فما من صباح إلا وعليها قطرة من قطر الجنة، فإذا أكلتموها فلا تنفضوها. قال: وقال أبو عبد الله (ع): وكان أبى (ع) ينهانا أن ننفضه إذا أكلناه.


6. From him, from Ali Bin Al Hakam, from Masny Bin Ziyad, Abu Abdullah (a.s.) has narrated that Amir Al-Momineen (a.s.) said: ‘Eat the endive for there is none from the mornings except that upon it would be a drop from the drops of the Paradise. So if you were to eat these, do not wipe it’. Abu Abdullah (a.s.) said: ‘My (a.s.) father (a.s.) forbade us (a.s.) to wipe it when we ate it’.


Grading:

The gradings provided are observations of the chain of narrators, a hadith with a weak or unknown chain may still be correct, and a hadith with a reliable chain may still be incorrect.

Gradings may also differ depending on the methodology of the scholar, for example Allamah Baqir al-Majlisi's gradings would be more lenient compared to Shaykh Asif al-Mohseni's.
