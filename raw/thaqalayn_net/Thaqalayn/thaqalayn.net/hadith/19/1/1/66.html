1. No name - Hadith #66

Back to chapter

عنه، عن أبيه، عن أبى إسماعيل، قال: سأل رجل شريكا ونحن حضور، فقال: ما تقول في رجل على باب داره مسجد لا يقنت فيه ووراء ذلك المسجد مسجد يقنت فيه؟ - قال: يأتي المسجد الذى يقنت فيه، فقال: ما تقول في رجل يرى القنوت فسها ولم يقنت؟ - قال: يسجد سجدتي السهو، فقال: ما تقول في رجل لم ير القنوت فسها فقنت؟ - قال: فضحك وقال: هذا رجل سها فأصاب


66. From him, from his father, from Abu Ismail who said, ‘What are you (a.s.) saying regarding a man, upon the door of his house is a Masjid, they do not ‘القنوت’ invocation (Qunoot) in it, and behind that Masjid there is a Masjid in which ‘القنوت’ invocation (Qunoot) is done in it?’ The Imam (a.s.) said: ‘He should come to the Masjid in which there is ‘القنوت’ invocation (Qunoot) done’. So he said, ‘What are you (a.s.) saying regarding a man who sees the ‘القنوت’ invocation, so he forgets it and does not offer ‘القنوت’?’ The Imam (a.s.) said: ‘He should perform two Prostrations of forgetfulness (Sahw)’. So he said, ‘What are you (a.s.) saying regarding a man who sees the ‘القنوت’ invocation, so he forgets it, but then he offers ‘القنوت’ (upon remembering it)?’ The Imam (a.s.) laughed and said: ‘This is a man who forgot, so he got it right’.


Grading:

The gradings provided are observations of the chain of narrators, a hadith with a weak or unknown chain may still be correct, and a hadith with a reliable chain may still be incorrect.

Gradings may also differ depending on the methodology of the scholar, for example Allamah Baqir al-Majlisi's gradings would be more lenient compared to Shaykh Asif al-Mohseni's.
