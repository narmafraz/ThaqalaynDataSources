101. The Masjids in Kufa - Hadith #3

Back to chapter

3ـ مُحَمَّدُ بْنُ يَحْيَى عَنْ مُحَمَّدِ بْنِ الْحُسَيْنِ عَنْ صَفْوَانَ بْنِ يَحْيَى عَنْ بَعْضِ أَصْحَابِنَا عَنْ أَبِي عَبْدِ الله (عَلَيْهِ الْسَّلام) قَالَ إِنَّ أَمِيرَ الْمُؤْمِنِينَ (صلّى اللهُ عَلَيْهِ وَآلِه) نَهَى بِالْكُوفَةِ عَنِ الصَّلاَةِ فِي خَمْسَةِ مَسَاجِدَ مَسْجِدِ الأشْعَثِ بْنِ قَيْسٍ وَمَسْجِدِ جَرِيرِ بْنِ عَبْدِ الله الْبَجَلِيِّ وَمَسْجِدِ سِمَاكِ بْنِ مَخْرَمَةَ وَمَسْجِدِ شَبَثِ بْنِ رِبْعِيٍّ وَمَسْجِدِ التَّيْمِ. وَفِي رِوَايَةِ أَبِي بَصِيرٍ مَسْجِدِ بَنِي السِّيدِ وَمَسْجِدِ بَنِي عَبْدِ الله بْنِ دَارِمٍ وَمَسْجِدِ غَنِيٍّ وَمَسْجِدِ سِمَاكٍ وَمَسْجِدِ ثَقِيفٍ وَمَسْجِدِ الأشْعَثِ.


3. Muhammad ibn Yahya has narrated from al-Husayn ibn Safwan ibn Yahya from certain individuals of our people who has said the following: “Abu ‘Abd Allah, ‘Alayhi al-Salam, has said that ’Amir al-Mu’minin, ‘Alayhi al-Salam, prohibited performing Salah (prayer) in five Masjids in Kufah, such as al-Ash‘ath ibn Qays’ Masjid, Jarir ibn ‘Abd Allah al-Bajaliy’s Masjid, Sammak ibn Makhruma’s Masjid, Shabath ibn Rib‘i’s Masjid and al-Taym’s Masjid.” According to a Hadith from abu Basir, one such Masjid is banu al-Sayyid Masjid, banu ‘Abd Allah ibn Darime Masjid, Ghany Masjid, Sammak’ Masjid, Thaqif’s Masjid and al-Ash‘ath’s Masjid.”


Grading:

Allamah Baqir al-Majlisi: مرسل كالصحيح وآخره مرسل - Mir‘at al ‘Uqul Fi Sharh Akhbar Al al Rasul (5/486)

The gradings provided are observations of the chain of narrators, a hadith with a weak or unknown chain may still be correct, and a hadith with a reliable chain may still be incorrect.

Gradings may also differ depending on the methodology of the scholar, for example Allamah Baqir al-Majlisi's gradings would be more lenient compared to Shaykh Asif al-Mohseni's.
