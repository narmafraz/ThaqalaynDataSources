49. The eating and the drinking with the left hand

Back to book

- عنه، عن عثمان بن عيسى، عن سماعة بن مهران، عن أبى عبد الله (ع) قال: سألته عن الرجل يأكل بشماله أو يشرب بها؟ قال: لا يأكل بشماله، ولا يشرب بشماله ولا يناول بها شيئا.


1. From him, from Usman Bin Isa, from Sama’at Bin Mahran, ‘Abu Abdullah (a.s.), said, ‘I asked about the man who eats with his left hand or drinks with it?’ The Imam (a.s.) said: ‘He should not eat with his left hand, nor drink with his left hand, nor take anything by it’.

2. From him, from his father, form Al Nazar Bin Suweyd, from Al Qasim Bin Suleyman, from Jarah Al Madainy, (It has been narrated) from Abu Abdullah (a.s.) that he (a.s.) disliked the man eating with his left hand or drinking, or taking (anything) by it’.
