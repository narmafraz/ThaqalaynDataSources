10. Taking the slaves and the maids (Slave girls)

Back to book

- عنه، عن أبيه، عن ابن أبى عمير، عن سيف بن عميرة وسليمة صاحب - السابرى، عن زيد الشحام، عن أبى عبد الله (ع) أن عليا (ع) أعتق ألف مملوك من كديده.


1. From him, from his father, from Ibn Abu Umeyr, from Sayf Bin Umeyra, and Saleyma owner of Al Sabiry, from Zayd Al Shahaam, (It has been narrated) from Abu Abdullah (a.s.), that Ali (a.s.) emancipated (freed) a thousand slaves from his (a.s.) ‘Kadeed’ (a land bigger than a valley)’.

Hadith to report:

1. From him, from his father, from Ibn Abu Umeyr, from Sayf Bin Umeyra, and Saleyma owner of Al Sabiry, from Zayd Al Shahaam, (It has been narrated) from Abu Abdullah (a.s.), that Ali (a.s.) emancipated (freed) a thousand slaves from his (a.s.) ‘Kadeed’ (a land bigger than a valley)’.


عنه، عن محسن بن أحمد، عن أبان بن عثمان، عن محمد بن مروان، عن أبى عبد الله (ع) أن أبا جعفر (ع) مات وترك ستين مملوكا فأعتق ثلثهم عند موته.


2. From him, from Mohsin Bin Ahmad, from Aban Bin Usman, from Muhammad Bin Marwan, (It has been narrated) from Abu Abdullah (a.s.) that Abu Ja’far (a.s.) passed away and left sixty slaves, so I (a.s.) emancipated (freed) a third of them at his (a.s.) passing away’.

2. From him, from Mohsin Bin Ahmad, from Aban Bin Usman, from Muhammad Bin Marwan, (It has been narrated) from Abu Abdullah (a.s.) that Abu Ja’far (a.s.) passed away and left sixty slaves, so I (a.s.) emancipated (freed) a third of them at his (a.s.) passing away’.


عنه، عن أبيه، عن ابن أبى عمير، عن مروان، قال: قال لى عبد الله بن أبى عبد الله (ع): اشتر لى غلاما رفا لهذا الامر يقوم في ضيعتي يكون فيها، قال: فقال أبو الحسن (ع): صلاحه لنفسه ولكن اشتر له مملوكا قويا يكون في ضيعته، قال: فقال: اشتر ما يقول لك.


3. From him, from his father, from Ibn Abu Umeyr, from Marwaan who said, ‘Abdullah son of Abu Abdullah (a.s.) said to me: ‘Buy a slave for me who would deal with this matter which is in my village and stays in it’. He said: ‘Abu Al-Hassan (a.s.) said: ‘His goodness is for himself, but buy for him a strong slave who would be in his village’. He said, ‘So he said, ‘Buy what he (a.s.) told you to’.

He said: ‘Abu Al-Hassan (a.s.) said: ‘His goodness is for himself, but buy for him a strong slave who would be in his village’. He said, ‘So he said, ‘Buy what he (a.s.) told you to’.


عنه، عن أبيه، عن صفوان بن يحيى، عن أبى مخلد السراج، قال: قال أبو عبد الله (ع) لاسماعيل حبيبه وحارث البصري: أطلبوا لى جارية من هذا الذى يسمونها " كدبانوجة " مسلمة تكون مع أم فروة، فدلوه على جارية كانت لشريك لابي من السراجين فولدت له بنتا وما ولدها، فأخبروه بخبرها، فاشتروها وحملوها إليه. وكان اسمها رسالة فحول اسمها فسماها سلمى وزوجها سالم.


4. From him, from his father, from Safwan Bin Yahya, from Abu Makhlad the saddler who said, ‘Abu Abdullah (a.s.) said to Ismail his (a.s.) beloved friend and Haris Al-Basry: ‘Seek out a maid from this which they refer to it as ‘Kadbanouja’, a Muslim woman, she can be with Umm Farwa’. So I enquired over a maid, and there was a partner of my father from the saddler, so there was born a daughter unto him and there was not child for her. So I informed him (a.s.) of her news. So I bought her and carried her over to him (a.s.). And her name was Rasaalat, so her name was changed and she was named as Salma, and married her to Saalim’.

عنه، عن أبيه، عن ابن أبى عمير، عن حماد بن عثمان، عن أبى عبد الله (ع) قال: إذا عمى الغلام عتق.


5. From him, from his father, from Ibn Abu Umeyr, from Hamaad Bin Usman, (It has been narrated) from Abu Abdullah (a.s.) having said: ‘If the slave is blind, emancipate (free) him’.
