2. Water of Zamzam

Back to book

- عنه، عن جعفر بن محمد، عن ابن القداح، عن أبى عبد الله، عن أبيه، قال: قال أمير المؤمنين (ع): ماء زمزم خير ماء على وجه الارض، وشر ماء على وجه الارض ماء برهوت التى بحضرموت ترده هام الكفار بالليل.


1. From him, from Ja’far Bin Muhammad, from Ibn Al Qadah, (It has been narrated) from Abu Abdullah (a.s.), from his (a.s.) father (a.s.) having said: ‘Amir Al-Momineen (a.s.) said: ‘Water of Zamzam is the best water upon the face of the earth, and the most evil water upon the face of the eat is water of Barhout which is at Hazramout (near Yemen) reducing the thirst of the Infidels at night’.

Hadith to report:

1. From him, from Ja’far Bin Muhammad, from Ibn Al Qadah, (It has been narrated) from Abu Abdullah (a.s.), from his (a.s.) father (a.s.) having said: ‘Amir Al-Momineen (a.s.) said: ‘Water of Zamzam is the best water upon the face of the earth, and the most evil water upon the face of the eat is water of Barhout which is at Hazramout (near Yemen) reducing the thirst of the Infidels at night’.


عنه، عن ابن القداح، عن أبى عبد الله، عن أبيه (ع) قال: قال رسول الله صلى الله عليه وآله: ماء زمزم دواء لما شرب له.


2. From him, from Ibn Al Qadah, (It has been narrated) from Abu Abdullah (a.s.), from his (a.s.) father (a.s.) having said: ‘Rasool-Allah (saww) said: ‘Water of Zamzam is a medication when it is drunk’.

2. From him, from Ibn Al Qadah, (It has been narrated) from Abu Abdullah (a.s.), from his (a.s.) father (a.s.) having said: ‘Rasool-Allah (saww) said: ‘Water of Zamzam is a medication when it is drunk’.


عنه، عن أبيه، عن محمد بن سنان، عن إسماعيل بن جابر، قال: سمعت أبا - عبد الله (ع) يقول: ماء زمزم شفاء من كل داء (وأظنه قال: " كائنا ما كان ") قال: وعرضت أنا هذا الحديث عن المبارك.


3. From him, from his father, from Muhammad Bin Sinan, from Ismail Bin Jabir who said, ‘I heard Abu Abdullah (a.s.) saying: ‘Water of Zamzam is a healing from every illness’ (and he thinks he (a.s.) said: ‘Wherever it may be’). He (the narrator) said, ‘And I was presented this Hadeeth from Al-Mubarak’.

Hadith to report:

3. From him, from his father, from Muhammad Bin Sinan, from Ismail Bin Jabir who said, ‘I heard Abu Abdullah (a.s.) saying: ‘Water of Zamzam is a healing from every illness’ (and he thinks he (a.s.) said: ‘Wherever it may be’). He (the narrator) said, ‘And I was presented this Hadeeth from Al-Mubarak’.


عنه، عن ابن فضال، عن على بن عقبة، عمن ذكره، عن أبى عبد الله (ع) قال: كانت زمزم أشد بياضا من اللبن، وأحلى من الشهد، وكانت سائحة فبغت على المياه، فأغارها الله وأجرى عليها عينا من صبر، وباسناده قال: ذكرت زمزم عند أبى عبد الله (ع) فقال: تجرى إليها عين من تحت الحجر، فإذا غلب ماء العين عذب ماء زمزم.


4. From him, from Ibn Fazal, from Ali Bin Uqba, from the one who mentioned it, Abu Abdullah (a.s.) has said: ‘(The water of) Zamzam used to be whiter than the milk, and sweeter than the honey, and it was travelling and came over the waters surprisingly. So Allah (azwj) Protected its honour and Caused a spring of patience to flow upon it’. And by his chain, said, ‘Zamzam was mentioned in the presence of Abu Abdullah (a.s.), so he (a.s.) said: ‘A spring flowed to it from beneath the (Black) Stone. So when the water of the spring overcame, it freshened the water of Zamzam’.

عنه، عن جعفر، عن ابن القداح، عن أبى عبد الله، عن أبيه (ع) ان النبي صلى الله عليه وآله كان يستهدى ماء زمزم وهو بالمدينة.


5. From him, from Ja’far, from Ibn Al Qadah, (It has been narrated) from Abu Abdullah (a.s.), from his (a.s.) father (a.s.), that the Prophet (saww) was Gifted the water of Zamzam and he (saww) was at Al-Medina’.

Hadith to report:

5. From him, from Ja’far, from Ibn Al Qadah, (It has been narrated) from Abu Abdullah (a.s.), from his (a.s.) father (a.s.), that the Prophet (saww) was Gifted the water of Zamzam and he (saww) was at Al-Medina’.


عنه، عن بعض أصحابنا رفعه قال: إذا شربت ماء زمزم فقل " اللهم اجعله علما نافعا، ورزقا واسعا، وشفاءا من كل داء وسقم ". وكان أبو الحسن (ع) يقول إذا شرب من زمزم: " بسم الله، الحمد لله، الشكر لله ".


6. From him, from one of our companions, raising it, said, ‘(He (a.s.) the Imam (a.s.) said): ‘Whenever you drink the water of Zamzam, so say: ‘اللهم اجعله علما نافعا، ورزقا واسعا، وشفاءا من كل داء وسقم’ ‘O Allah (azwj)! Make it to be thoroughly beneficial, and extensive in sustenance, and a healing from every disease and illness’ And Abu Al-Hassan (a.s.) was saying when he (a.s.) drank from Zamzam: ‘بسم الله، الحمد لله، الشكر لله’ ‘In the Name of Allah (azwj), the Praise is for Allah (azwj), and ‘Thanks’ is for Allah (azwj)’.
