35. The Onions

Back to book

- عنه، عن أبيه، عن أحمد بن بن النضر، عن عمرو بن شمر، عن جابر، قال قال أبو عبد الله (ع): البصل يذهب بالنصب ويشد العصب، ويزيد في الماء والخطا، ويذهب بالحمى.


1. From him, from his father, from Ahmad Bin Al Nazar, from Amro Bin Shimr, from Jabir who said, ‘Abu Abdullah (a.s.) said: ‘The onions remove the tiredness and strengthen the nerves, and increases in the water (semen) and the strength, and eliminate the fever’.

Hadith to report:

1. From him, from his father, from Ahmad Bin Al Nazar, from Amro Bin Shimr, from Jabir who said, ‘Abu Abdullah (a.s.) said: ‘The onions remove the tiredness and strengthen the nerves, and increases in the water (semen) and the strength, and eliminate the fever’.


عنه، عن السيارى، عن أحمد خالد، عن أحمد بن المبارك الدينورى، عن أبى عثمان، عن درست، عن أبى عبد الله (ع) قال: البصل يطيب الفم، ويشد الظهر، ويرق البشرة.


2. From him, from Al Sayyari, from Ahmad Khalid, from Ahmad Bin Al Mubarak Al Daymouri, from Abu Usman, from Darsat, (It has been narrated) from Abu Abdullah (a.s.) having said: ‘The onion gives flavour to the mouth, and strengthens the back, and smoothens the skin’.

Hadith to report:

2. From him, from Al Sayyari, from Ahmad Khalid, from Ahmad Bin Al Mubarak Al Daymouri, from Abu Usman, from Darsat, (It has been narrated) from Abu Abdullah (a.s.) having said: ‘The onion gives flavour to the mouth, and strengthens the back, and smoothens the skin’.


عنه، عن منصور بن العباس، عن عبد العزيز بن حسان البغدادي، عن صالح بن عقبة، عن عبد الله بن محمد الجعفي، قال: ذكر أبو عبد الله (ع) البصل فقال: يطيب النكهة، ويذهب بالبلغم، ويزيد في الجماع. وفى حديث آخر قال: قال أبو عبد الله (ع): كلوا البصل فان فيه ثلاث خصال، يطيب النكهة، ويشد اللثة، ويزيد في الماء.


3. From him, from Mansour Bin Al Abbas, from Abdul Aziz Bin Hasaan Al Baghdady, from Salih Bin Uqba, from Abdullah Bin Muhammad Al Ju’fy who said, ‘Abu Abdullah (a.s.) mentioned the onion, so he (a.s.) said: ‘It aromatises the flavour, and eliminates the phlegm, and increases in the copulation’. And in another Hadeeth, said, ‘Abu Abdullah (a.s.) said: ‘Eat the onions, for therein are three qualities – It aromatises the flavour, and strengthens the gums, and increases in the water (semen)’.

عنه، عن محمد بن على، عن محمد بن الفضيل، عن عبد الرحمن بن يزيد بن أسلم، عن أبى عبد الله (ع) قال: قال رسول الله صلى الله عليه وآله: إذا دخلتم بلدا فكلوا من بصلها يطرد عنكم وباؤها.


4. From him, from Muhammad Bin Ali, from Muhammad Bin Al Fazeyl, from Abul Rahman Bin Yazeed Bin Aslam, (It has been narrated) from Abu Abdullah (a.s.) having said: ‘Rasool-Allah (saww) said: ‘When you enter a city, so eat from its onions, its plagues would be repulsed from you’.
