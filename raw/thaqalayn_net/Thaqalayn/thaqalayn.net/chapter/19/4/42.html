42. The Truffle

Back to book

- عنه، عن النوفلي، عن عيسى بن عبد الله الهاشمي، عن إبراهيم بن عبد الله الهاشمي، عن إبراهيم بن على الرافعى، عن أبى عبد الله (ع) قال: قال رسول الله صلى الله عليه وآله: الكمأة من نبت الجنة، وماؤها نافع من وجع العين.


1. From him, from Al Nowfaly, from Isa Bin Abdullah Al Hashimy, from Ibrahim Bin Abdullah Al Hashim, form Ibrahim Bin Ali Al Raf’ie, (It has been narrated) from Abu Abdullah (a.s.) having said: ‘Rasool-Allah (saww) said: ‘The truffles are from the vegetation of the Paradise, and its water is beneficial for sore eyes’.

Hadith to report:

1. From him, from Al Nowfaly, from Isa Bin Abdullah Al Hashimy, from Ibrahim Bin Abdullah Al Hashim, form Ibrahim Bin Ali Al Raf’ie, (It has been narrated) from Abu Abdullah (a.s.) having said: ‘Rasool-Allah (saww) said: ‘The truffles are from the vegetation of the Paradise, and its water is beneficial for sore eyes’.


عنه، عن محمد بن على، عن محمد بن الفضيل، عن عبد الرحمان بن زيد بن أسلم، عن أبى عبد الله (ع) قال: قال رسول الله صلى الله عليه وآله: الكمأة من المن، والمن من الجنة، وماؤها شفاء للعين.


2. From him, from Muhammad Bin Ali, from Muhammad Bin Al Fazeyl, from Abdul Rahman Bin Zayd Bin Aslam, (It has been narrated) from Abu Abdullah (a.s.) having said: ‘Rasool-Allah (saww) said: ‘The truffle is from the Manna, and the Manna is from the Paradise, and its water is a healing for the eyes’.

Hadith to report:

2. From him, from Muhammad Bin Ali, from Muhammad Bin Al Fazeyl, from Abdul Rahman Bin Zayd Bin Aslam, (It has been narrated) from Abu Abdullah (a.s.) having said: ‘Rasool-Allah (saww) said: ‘The truffle is from the Manna, and the Manna is from the Paradise, and its water is a healing for the eyes’.


عنه، عن على بن الحكم، عن أبان بن عثمان، عن أبى بصير، عن فاطمة بنت على، عن أمامة بنت أبى العاص بن الربيع، وأمها زينب بنت رسول الله صلى الله عليه وآله قالت: أتانى أمير المؤمنين (ع) في شهر رمضان، فأتى بعشاء وتمر وكمأة فأكل وكان يحب الكمأة.


3. From him, from Ali Bin Al Hakam, from Aban Bin Usman, from Abu Baseer, (It has been narrated) from Syeda Fatima (a.s.) daughter of Ali (a.s.), from Amamat Bin Abu Al Aas Bin Al Rabi’e, and her mother Zaynab, daughter of Rasool-Allah (saww) who said, ‘Amir Al-Momineen (a.s.) paid me a visit in the Month of Ramazan, so I came with the dinner, and dates and truffles. So he (a.s.) ate, and he (a.s.) was liking the truffles’.
