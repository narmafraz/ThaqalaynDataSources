5. Al-Khal (A vinegar paste)

Back to book

- عنه، عن محمد بن على، عن ابن أبى عمير، عن هشام بن سالم الجواليقى، عن سليمان بن خالد، عن أبى عبد الله (ع) قال: الخل يشد العقل.


1. From him, from Muhammad Bin Ali, from Ibn Abu Umeyr, from Hisham Bin Salim Al Jawalayqi, from Suleyman Bin Khalid, Abu Abdullah (a.s.) has said: ‘Al-Khal intensifies the intellect’.

5. From him, from Al Washa, from Ibn Sinan, (It has been narrated) from Abu Abdullah (a.s.) having said: ‘Rasool-Allah (saww) came to Umm Salma (as), so she (as) forwarded nearer to him (saww) some morsels, so he (saww) said: ‘Is there curry with you (as)?’ She said: ‘No, O Rasool-Allah (saww)! There is not with me (as) except for Khal’. So he (saww) said: ‘The best of the curries is Al-Khal. A House in which is Al-Khal is not empty’.

18. From him, from Muhammad Bin Al Al Hamdany that, ‘A man was in the presence of Abu Al-Hassan Al-Ridha (a.s.) at Khorasan, so a meal was presented to him (a.s.) wherein was Khal and salt, so he (a.s.) began with Al-Khal. So the man said, ‘May I be sacrificed for you (a.s.)! You (a.s.) had ordered us to begin with the salt’. So he (a.s.) said: ‘This is similar to this (meaning Al-Khal). It intensifies the mind and increases the intellect’.
