22. The Endive

Back to book

- عنه، عن أبى عبد الله السيارى، عن أحمد بن الفضل، عن محمد بن سعيد، عن أبى جميلة، عن جابر، عن أبى جعفر (ع) قال: الهندبا شجرة، على باب الجنة.


1. From him, from Abu Abdullah Al Sattari, from Ahmad Bin Al Fazal, from Muhammad Bin Saeed, from Abu Jameela, from Jabir, Abu Ja’far (a.s.) has said: ‘The endive tree grows at the Gate of the Paradise’.

6. So if you were to eat these, do not wipe it’. Abu Abdullah (a.s.) said: ‘My (a.s.) father (a.s.) forbade us (a.s.) to wipe it when we ate it’.

So eat it and do not wipe it during its eating’. He (a.s.) said: ‘My (a.s.) father (a.s.) forbade us (a.s.) from wiping it when we (a.s.) ate it’.
