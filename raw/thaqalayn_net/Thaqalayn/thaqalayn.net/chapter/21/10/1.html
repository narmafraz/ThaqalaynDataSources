1. Regarding the imams (asws), they know the pact from rasool-allah (saww) in the bequest to the ones from after him (saww)

Back to book

حَدَّثَنَا بأَحْمَدُ بْنُ مُحَمَّدٍ عَنِ ابْنِ أَبِي عُمَيْرٍ عَنْ حَمَّادِ بْنِ عُثْمَانَ عَنْ عَمْرِو بْنِ الْأَشْعَثِ قَالَ سَمِعْتُ أَبَا عَبْدِ اللَّهِ ع يَقُولُ‏ أَ تَرَوْنَ الْمُوصِيَ مِنَّا يُوصِي إِلَى مَنْ يُرِيدُ لَا وَ اللَّهِ وَ لَكِنَّهُ عَهْدٌ مِنْ رَسُولِ اللَّهِ ص رَجُلٌ فَرَجُلٌ حَتَّى يَنْتَهِيَ الْأَمْرُ إِلَى صَاحِبِهِ‏.


1. It is narrated to us by Ahmad Bin Muhammad, from Ibn Abu Umeyr, from Hammad Bin Usman, from Amro Bin Al Ash’as who said, ‘Are you viewing the bequeathed one (a.s.) from us (a.s.) can bequeath to one he (a.s.) wants? No, by Allah (azwj)! But it is a pact from Rasool-Allah (saww), a man so a man, until the command ends up to its (rightful) Master (a.s.)’’.

2. It is narrated to us by Ahmad Bin Muhammad, from Abdullah Al Hajjal, from Dawood Bin Yazeed, from the one who mentioned it, ‘From Abu Abdullah (a.s.) having said: ‘Are you view the command is up to us (a.s.) that we (a.s.) place it in the one we (a.s.) so desire to? Never! By Allah (azwj) it is a pact from Rasool-Allah (saww) to Ali (a.s.) Bin Abu Talib (a.s.), a man for a man, until it ends up to the Master (a.s.) of this command’’.

3.
