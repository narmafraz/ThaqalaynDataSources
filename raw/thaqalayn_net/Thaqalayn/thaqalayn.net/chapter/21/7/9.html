9. Regarding the imams (asws), they (asws) are speaking upon seventy perspectives, all of these have a way out, and they (asws) are issuing fatwas by that

Back to book

حَدَّثَنَا مُحَمَّدُ بْنُ الْحُسَيْنِ عَنِ النَّضْرِ بْنِ شُعَيْبٍ عَنْ عَبْدِ الْغَفَّارِ الْجَازِيِّ عَنْ أَبِي عَبْدِ اللَّهِ ع أَنَّهُ قَالَ: إِنِّي لَأَتَكَلَّمُ عَلَى سَبْعِينَ وَجْهاً لِي فِي كُلِّهَا الْمَخْرَجُ.


1. It is narrated to us by Muhammad Bin AL Husayn, from Al Nazar Bin Shuayb, from Abdul Gaffar Al Jazy, ‘From Abu Abdullah (a.s.) having said: ‘I (a.s.) tend to speak upon seventy perspective, there is the way out for me (a.s.) in all of these’’.

حَدَّثَنَا أَحْمَدُ بْنُ مُحَمَّدٍ عَنْ مُحَمَّدِ بْنِ إِسْمَاعِيلَ عَنْ عَلِيِّ بْنِ النُّعْمَانِ عَنِ ابْنِ مُسْكَانَ عَنْ عَبْدِ الْأَعْلَى بْنِ أَعْيَنَ قَالَ: دَخَلْتُ أَنَا وَ عَلِيُّ بْنُ حَنْظَلَةَ عَلَى أَبِي عَبْدِ اللَّهِ ع فَسَأَلَهُ عَلِيُّ بْنُ حَنْظَلَةَ عَنْ مَسْأَلَةٍ فَأَجَابَ فِيهَا فَقَالَ رَجُلٌ‏ فَإِنْ كَانَ كَذَا وَ كَذَا فَأَجَابَهُ فِيهَا بِوَجْهٍ آخَرَ وَ إِنْ كَانَ كَذَا وَ كَذَا فَأَجَابَهُ بِوَجْهٍ‏ حَتَّى أَجَابَهُ فِيهَا بِأَرْبَعَةِ وُجُوهٍ فَالْتَفَتَ إِلَيَّ عَلِيُّ بْنُ حَنْظَلَةَ قَالَ يَا أَبَا مُحَمَّدٍ قَدْ أَحْكَمْنَاهُ فَسَمِعَهُ أَبُو عَبْدِ اللَّهِ فَقَالَ لَا تَقُلْ هَكَذَا يَا أَبَا الْحَسَنِ فَإِنَّكَ رَجُلٌ وَرِعٌ إِنَّ مِنَ الْأَشْيَاءِ أَشْيَاءَ ضَيِّقَةً وَ لَيْسَ تَجْرِي إِلَّا عَلَى وَجْهٍ وَاحِدٍ مِنْهَا وَقْتُ الْجُمُعَةِ لَيْسَ لِوَقْتِهَا إِلَّا وَاحِدٌ حِينَ تَزُولُ الشَّمْسُ وَ مِنَ الْأَشْيَاءِ أَشْيَاءُ مُوَسَّعَةٌ تَجْرِي عَلَى وُجُوهٍ كَثِيرَةٍ وَ هَذَا مِنْهَا وَ اللَّهِ إِنَّ لَهُ عِنْدِي سَبْعِينَ وَجْهاً.


2. It is narrated to us by Ahmad Bin Muhammad, from Muhammad Bin Ismail, from Ali Bin Al Numan, from Ibn Muskan, from Abdul A’ala Bin Ayn who said, ‘I and Ali Bin Hanzala entered to see Abu Abdullah. Ali Bin Hanzala asked him (a.s.) about an issue, and he (a.s.) answered regarding it. A man said, ‘Supposing it was such and such?’ So, he (a.s.) answered him in another perspective. (He said), ‘And if it was such and such?’ He (a.s.) answered him in (another) perspective, to the extent that he (a.s.) answered him with four perspectives. Ali Bin Hanzala turned towards me and said, ‘O Abu Muhammad! We have its judgment’. Abu Abdullah (a.s.) said: ‘Do not speak like this, O Abu Al Hassan, for you are a devout man. From the things there are narrow things, and these cannot flow except upon one perspective. From these is the timing of the Friday (Salat). There isn’t any timing for it except one, when the sun starts to decline (midday). And from the things there are things with leeway, flowing upon many perspectives, and this is from it. By Allah (azwj), there are seventy perspectives for it with me (a.s.)’’.

3. It is narrated to us by Abdullah, from Al hassan Bin Al Husayn Al Luluie, from Ibn Sinan, from Ali Bin Abu Hamza who said, ‘I and Abu Baseer entered to see Abu Abdullah (a.s.). While we were seated when Abu Abdullah (a.s.) spoke a phrase. I said within myself, ‘This is from what I shall carry to the Shias. By Allah (azwj)! This is a Hadeeth I have not heard the like of it, at all!’ He (Abu Baseer) said, ‘He (a.s.) looked at my (a.s.) face, then said: ‘I (a.s.) tend to speak with the one phrase, there are seventy perspectives in it for me (a.s.). If I (a.s.) like I (a.s.) can take is as such, and if I (a.s.) like, I (a.s.) can take is as such’’.

It is narrated to us by Ahmad Bin Muhammad, from Al Husayn Bin Saeed, from fazala Bin Ayoub, and Ali Bin Al Hakam, altogether from Umar Bin Aban Al Kalby, from Udeym brother of Ayoub, from Humran Bin Ayn, ‘From Abu Abdullah (a.s.), he (the narrator) said, ‘I heard him (a.s.) saying: ‘I tend to speak upon seventy perspectives. For me (a.s.), in all of these, is the way out’’.


حَدَّثَنَا أَحْمَدُ بْنُ مُحَمَّدٍ عَنْ عَبْدِ الرَّحْمَنِ بْنِ أَبِي نَجْرَانَ عَنْ مُحَمَّدِ بْنِ حُمْرَانَ عَنْ مُحَمَّدِ بْنِ مُسْلِمٍ قَالَ قَالَ أَبُو عَبْدِ اللَّهِ‏ إِنِّي أَتَكَلَّمُ عَلَى سَبْعِينَ وَجْهاً لِي مِنْهَا الْمَخْرَجُ.


15. It is narrated to us by Ahmad Bin Muhammad, from Abdul Rahman Bin Abu Najran, from Mihammad Bin Humran, from Muhammad Bin Muslim who said, ‘Abu Abdullah (a.s.) said: ‘I (a.s.) speak upon seventy perspectives. For me (a.s.) is the way out from these’’.
