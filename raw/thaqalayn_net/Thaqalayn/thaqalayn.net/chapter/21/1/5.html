5. The people are categorised upon three – a scholar, and a student, and scum, and that the imams (asws) from progeny (asws) of muhammad (saww), they (asws) are the scholar, and their (asws) shias are the students, and rest of the people are scum

حَدَّثَنَا إِبْرَاهِيمُ بْنُ هَاشِمٍ عَنْ يَحْيَى بْنِ أَبِي عِمْرَانَ عَنْ يُونُسَ عَنْ جَمِيلٍ قَالَ سَمِعْتُ أَبَا عَبْدِ اللَّهِ ع يَقُولُ‏ يَغْدُو النَّاسُ عَلَى ثَلَاثَةِ صُنُوفٍ عَالِمٍ وَ مُتَعَلِّمٍ وَ غُثَاءٍ فَنَحْنُ الْعُلَمَاءُ وَ شِيعَتُنَا الْمُتَعَلِّمُونَ وَ سَائِرُ النَّاسِ غُثَاءٌ.


1. It is narrated to us by Ibrahim Bin Hashim, from Yahya Bin Abu Imran, from Yunus, from Jameel who said, ‘I heard Abu Abdullah (a.s.) saying: ‘The people are categorised upon three types – a scholar, and a student, and scum. So, we (a.s.) are the scholars, and our (a.s.) Shias are the students, and rest of the people are scum’’.

2. So, we (a.s.) are the scholars, and our (a.s.) Shias are the students, and rest of the people are scum’’.
