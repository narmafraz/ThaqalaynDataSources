11. Remembering Allah (azwj) during the journey - Hadith #1

Back to chapter

- عنه، عن أبيه، عن محمد بن سنان، عن حذيفة بن منصور، قال: صحبت أبا عبد الله (ع) وهو متوجه إلى مكة، فلما صلى قال: " اللهم خل سبيلنا، وأحسن تسييرنا، وأحسن عاقبتنا "، وكلما صعد إلى أكمة قال: " اللهم لك الشرف على كل شرف.


1. From him, from his father, from Muhammad Bin Sinan, from Huzeyfa Bin Mansour who said, ‘I accompanied Abu Abdullah (a.s.) and he (a.s.) was going to Makkah. So when he (a.s.) Prayed, said: ‘O Allah (azwj)! Open our way for us, and Make good our journey, and Make good our health’. And every time he (a.s.) ascended a hill, said: ‘Our Allah (azwj)! For You (azwj) is the Nobility over all nobilities’.


Grading:

The gradings provided are observations of the chain of narrators, a hadith with a weak or unknown chain may still be correct, and a hadith with a reliable chain may still be incorrect.

Gradings may also differ depending on the methodology of the scholar, for example Allamah Baqir al-Majlisi's gradings would be more lenient compared to Shaykh Asif al-Mohseni's.
