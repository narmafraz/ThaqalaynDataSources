44. The dates - Hadith #47

Back to chapter

عنه، عن الحجال، عن أبى داود سليمان الحمار، قال: كنا عند أبى عبد الله (ع) فأتينا بقباع من رطب، فيه ألوان من التمر، فجعل يأخذ الواحدة بعد الواحدة وقال: " أي شئ تسمون هذه؟ - " حتى وضع يده على واحدة منها، قلت: نسميه المشان، قال: لكنا نسميه " أم جرذان " إن رسول الله صلى الله عليه وآله أتى بشئ منها، ودعا لها فليس شئ من نخل أجمل لما يؤخذ منها.


47. From him, from Al Hajaal, from Abu Dawood Suleyman Al Hamaar who said, ‘I was in the presence of Abu Abdullah (a.s.), so we came across a basket of dates, in which were various types of the dates. So he (a.s.) went on to grab one after the other and said: ‘Which thing do you call these with?’ - Until he (a.s.) placed his (a.s.) hand upon one from it, I said, ‘We call it Al-Mashaan’. The Imam (a.s.) said: ‘But we call it ‘Umm Jarzaan’. Rasool-Allah (saww) was given something form these, and called for it (to be eaten). Thus, there is nothing from the palm tree more beautiful than what he (saww) had taken from it’.


Grading:

The gradings provided are observations of the chain of narrators, a hadith with a weak or unknown chain may still be correct, and a hadith with a reliable chain may still be incorrect.

Gradings may also differ depending on the methodology of the scholar, for example Allamah Baqir al-Majlisi's gradings would be more lenient compared to Shaykh Asif al-Mohseni's.
