43. Forbidding from abundance of the food and eating too much - Hadith #7

Back to chapter

عنه، قال: حدثنى بكر بن صالح، عن جعفر بن محمد الهاشمي عن أبى - جعفر العطار، قال: سمعت جعفر بن محمد يحدث عن أبيه، عن جده (ع)، عن رسول الله صلى الله عليه وآله قال: قال جبرئيل (ع) في كلام بلغنيه عن ربى: يا محمد وأخرى هي الاولى والآخرة، يقول لك ربك: يا محمد ما أبغضت وعاء قط إلا بطنا ملآن.


7. From him, from Bakr Bin Salih, from Ja’far Bin Muhammad Al Hashimy, from Abu Ja’far Al Ataar who said, ‘I heard Ja’far Bin Muhammad (a.s.) narrate from his (a.s.) father (a.s.), from his (a.s.) grandfather (a.s.), from Rasool-Allah (saww) having said: ‘Jibraeel (as) said in a speech which he (as) delivered from my (saww) Lord (azwj): ‘O Muhammad (saww)! And another (thing) which is the first for the Hereafter, your (saww) Lord (azwj) is Saying to you (saww): “O Muhammad (saww)! I do not Hate an empty container at all except for an overfilled belly’.


Grading:

The gradings provided are observations of the chain of narrators, a hadith with a weak or unknown chain may still be correct, and a hadith with a reliable chain may still be incorrect.

Gradings may also differ depending on the methodology of the scholar, for example Allamah Baqir al-Majlisi's gradings would be more lenient compared to Shaykh Asif al-Mohseni's.
