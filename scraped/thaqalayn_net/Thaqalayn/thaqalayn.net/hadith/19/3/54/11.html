54. Such regarding what was in my possession from a copy of Al-Mahaasin - Hadith #11

Back to chapter

وعنه، عن أحمد بن محمد، عن الحسين بن خالد، قال: قلت لابي الحسن (ع): إن الناس يقولون: من لم يأكل اللحم ثلاثة أيام ساء خلقه، فقال: كذبوا، ولكن من لا - يأكل اللحم أربعين يوما تغير خلقه وبدنه وذلك لانتقال النطفة في مقدار أربعين يوما.


11. From him, from Ahmad Bin Muhammad, from Al Husayn Bin Khalid who said, ‘I said to Abu Al-Hassan (a.s.), ‘The people are saying: ‘The one who does not eat the meat for three days would become worn out’. So he (a.s.) said: ‘The are lying’ But, the one who does not eat the meat for forty days, his morals and his body would change, and that is the changing of the seed to the measure of forty days’.


Grading:

The gradings provided are observations of the chain of narrators, a hadith with a weak or unknown chain may still be correct, and a hadith with a reliable chain may still be incorrect.

Gradings may also differ depending on the methodology of the scholar, for example Allamah Baqir al-Majlisi's gradings would be more lenient compared to Shaykh Asif al-Mohseni's.
