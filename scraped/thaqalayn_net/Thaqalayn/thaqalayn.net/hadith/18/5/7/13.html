7. The analogist and the opinion - Hadith #13

Back to chapter

عنه، عن أبيه، عن محمد بن أبي عمير، عن محمد بن حكيم، قال: قلت لابي - الحسن موسى بن جعفر عليهما السلام: جعلت فداك فقهنا في الدين وأغنانا الله بكم عن الناس حتى أن الجماعة منا لتكون في المجلس ما يسأل رجل صاحبه يحضره المسألة ويحضره جوابها منا من الله علينا بكم فربما ورد علينا الشئ لم يأتنا فيه عنك ولا عن آبائك شئ فننظر إلى أحسن ما يحضرنا وأوفق الاشياء لما جائنا عنكم فنأخذ به؟ - فقال: هيهات هيهات! في ذلك والله هلك من هلك يا ابن حكيم ثم قال: لعن الله أبا حنيفة يقول: قال علي و قلت،


13. From him, from his father, from Muhammad Bin Umeyr, from Muhammad Bin Hakeem who said, ‘I said to Abu Al-Hassan Musa (a.s.) Bin Ja’far (a.s.), ‘May I be sacrficied for you (a.s.)! We ponder in the Religion, and Allah (azwj) has Made us to be needless from the people through you (a.s.), to the extent that a group of us have become such in the gathering that, a man present in the gathering does not ask a question, but an answer is present from us, which Allah (azwj) has Bestowed upon us through you (a.s.). However, sometimes such a thing is referred to us, that we have not been given anything about it, neither from you (a.s.), nor from your (a.s.) forefathers (a.s.). So we look at the best of what is present with us, and the most satisfactory things which have come to us from you (a.s.), so shall we take to it?’ So the Imam (a.s.) said: ‘Impossible! Impossible! By Allah (azwj)! It is with regards to that, was destroyed the one who was destroyed, O Ibn Hakeem. Then the Imam (a.s.) said: ‘May Allah (azwj) Curse Abu Haneefa for saying, ‘Ali (a.s.) spoke (this), and I speak (this)’ (implying my judgement is better than Ali (a.s.))


Grading:

The gradings provided are observations of the chain of narrators, a hadith with a weak or unknown chain may still be correct, and a hadith with a reliable chain may still be incorrect.

Gradings may also differ depending on the methodology of the scholar, for example Allamah Baqir al-Majlisi's gradings would be more lenient compared to Shaykh Asif al-Mohseni's.
