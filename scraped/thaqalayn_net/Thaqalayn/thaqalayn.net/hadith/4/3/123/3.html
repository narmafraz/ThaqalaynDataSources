123. Performing Tawaf and Touching the Corners - Hadith #3

Back to chapter

3ـ أَحْمَدُ بْنُ مُحَمَّدٍ عَنِ الْحُسَيْنِ بْنِ سَعِيدٍ عَنْ إِبْرَاهِيمَ بْنِ أَبِي الْبِلادِ عَنْ عَبْدِ السَّلامِ بْنِ عَبْدِ الرَّحْمَنِ بْنِ نُعَيْمٍ قَالَ قُلْتُ لأَبِي عَبْدِ الله (عَلَيْهِ السَّلاَم) دَخَلْتُ طَوَافَ الْفَرِيضَةِ فَلَمْ يُفْتَحْ لِي شَيْ‏ءٌ مِنَ الدُّعَاءِ إِلاَّ الصَّلاةُ عَلَى مُحَمَّدٍ وَآلِ مُحَمَّدٍ وَسَعَيْتُ فَكَانَ كَذَلِكَ فَقَالَ مَا أُعْطِيَ أَحَدٌ مِمَّنْ سَأَلَ أَفْضَلَ مِمَّا أُعْطِيتَ.


3. Ahmad ibn Muhammad has narrated from al-Husayn ibn Sa‘id from Ibrahim ibn abu al-Balad from ‘Abd al-Salam ibn ‘Abd al- Rahman ibn Nu’aym who has said the following: “I once said to Abu `Abd Allah, ‘Alayhi al-Salam, £ I began my obligatory Tawaf but did not know any prayer to say except, “O Allah, grant salawat (favors and compensation to Muhammad and his family worthy of their services to Your cause).” During Sa £ y also I said what I had said during Tawaf. He (the Imam) said, £ No one asking for help is given anything better than what is given to you.’”


Grading:

Allamah Baqir al-Majlisi: حسن - Mir‘at al ‘Uqul Fi Sharh Akhbar Al al Rasul (18/22)

The gradings provided are observations of the chain of narrators, a hadith with a weak or unknown chain may still be correct, and a hadith with a reliable chain may still be incorrect.

Gradings may also differ depending on the methodology of the scholar, for example Allamah Baqir al-Majlisi's gradings would be more lenient compared to Shaykh Asif al-Mohseni's.
