101. The Case of al-Mahsur (Prevented) and al-Masdud (Confined)(Those Prevented from Performing al-Hajj Due to Illness and So On or by Authorities) - Hadith #9

Back to chapter

9ـ حُمَيْدُ بْنُ زِيَادٍ عَنِ الْحَسَنِ بْنِ مُحَمَّدِ بْنِ سَمَاعَةَ عَنْ أَحْمَدَ بْنِ الْحَسَنِ الْمِيثَمِيِّ عَنْ أَبَانٍ عَنْ زُرَارَةَ عَنْ أَبِي جَعْفَرٍ (عَلَيْهِ السَّلاَم) قَالَ الْمَصْدُودُ يَذْبَحُ حَيْثُ صُدَّ وَيَرْجِعُ صَاحِبُهُ فَيَأْتِي النِّسَاءَ وَالْمَحْصُورُ يَبْعَثُ بِهَدْيِهِ وَيَعِدُهُمْ يَوْماً فَإِذَا بَلَغَ الْهَدْيُ أَحَلَّ هَذَا فِي مَكَانِهِ قُلْتُ لَهُ أَ رَأَيْتَ إِنْ رَدُّوا عَلَيْهِ دَرَاهِمَهُ وَلَمْ يَذْبَحُوا عَنْهُ وَقَدْ أَحَلَّ فَأَتَى النِّسَاءَ قَالَ فَلْيُعِدْ وَلَيْسَ عَلَيْهِ شَيْ‏ءٌ وَلْيُمْسِكِ الآنَ عَنِ النِّسَاءِ إِذَا بَعَثَ.


9. Al-Nadr ibn Suwayd has narrated from al-Hassan ibn Muhammad ibn Sama‘ah from Ahmad ibn al-Hassan al-Mithamiy from Aban from Zurarah who has said the following: “Abu Ja‘far, ‘Alayhi al-Salam, has said, ‘Al-Masdud (prevented) offers an animal for slaughtering wherever he is prevented and returns home. Women are lawful for him. Al-Mahsur (confined) sends his offering, sets up a certain time and when the offering reaches its place, he in his place comes out of the state of Ihram.’ I then asked, ‘What happens if they returned dirham and did not slaughter, but he has come out of the state of Ihram and has gone with women? He (the Imam) said, ‘He must do it again; there is nothing on him but for now he must stay away from women when he sends his offering.’”


Grading:

Allamah Baqir al-Majlisi: موثق - Mir‘at al ‘Uqul Fi Sharh Akhbar Al al Rasul (17/343)

The gradings provided are observations of the chain of narrators, a hadith with a weak or unknown chain may still be correct, and a hadith with a reliable chain may still be incorrect.

Gradings may also differ depending on the methodology of the scholar, for example Allamah Baqir al-Majlisi's gradings would be more lenient compared to Shaykh Asif al-Mohseni's.
