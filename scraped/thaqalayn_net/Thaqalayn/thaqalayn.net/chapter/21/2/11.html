11. Another regarding wilayah of the imams (asws)

Back to book

حَدَّثَنَا السِّنْدِيُّ بْنُ مُحَمَّدٍ عَنْ يُونُسَ بْنِ يَعْقُوبَ عَنْ عَبْدِ الْأَعْلَى قَالَ قَالَ أَبُو عَبْدِ اللَّهِ ع‏ مَا نُبِّئَ نَبِيٌّ قَطُّ إِلَّا بِمَعْرِفَةِ حَقِّنَا وَ بِفَضْلِنَا عَلَى مَنْ سِوَانَا.


1. It is narrated to us by Al Sindy Bin Muhammad, from Yunus Bin Yaqoub, from Abdul A’ala who said, ‘Abu Abdullah (a.s.) said: ‘A Prophet (as) was not Made a Prophet (as) at all except by recognising our (a.s.) rights and our (a.s.) merits over the ones besides us (a.s.)’’.

2. Wilayah, and by our (a.s.) grace from the ones besides us (a.s.)’’.
