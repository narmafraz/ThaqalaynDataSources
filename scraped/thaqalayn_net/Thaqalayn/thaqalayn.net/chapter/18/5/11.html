10. From him, from one of our companions, raising it, said, ‘Amir-ul-Momineen (a.s.) said: ‘I (a.s.) shall be describing Al-Islam today (in such a manner) that no one before me (a.s.) has describing it, nor will anyone after me (a.s.) describe it similarly to that. Al-Islam is the submission; and the submission, it is the conviction; and the conviction, it is the ratification; and the ratification, it is the acceptance; and the acceptance, it is the deed; and the deed, it is the performance of it. The Believer does not take his Religion from his opinion, but it is Given to him from his Lord (azwj), so he holds onto it. The Believer sees his conviction in his deeds, and the Infidel sees his denial in his deeds. So, by the One (azwj) in Whose Hand is my (a.s.) soul, they have not recognised the Command of their Lord (azwj). Therefore, learn a lesson from the denial of the Infidels, and the hypocrites, by their bad deeds’.

This is Your (azwj) servant, raising his hands to You (azwj), Asking You (azwj) for a need, and asks You (azwj) for Forgiveness since the last seven days, You (azwj) has not Answered him?’ The Imam (a.s.) said: ‘So Allah (azwj) Revealed unto him (as): “O Musa (as)! Even if he were to supplicate to Me (azwj) until his hands fall down, and his tongue is cut-off, I (azwj) will not Answer him until he comes to Me (azwj) from the Door which I (azwj) have Commanded him by (My (azwj) Prophet (as)/Imam (a.s.))”.
