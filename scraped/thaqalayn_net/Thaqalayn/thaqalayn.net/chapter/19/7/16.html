4. From him, from Ibn Mahboub, from Muhammad Bin Marid who said, ‘I heard Abu Abdullah (a.s.) saying: ‘There is none from a Believer who has a dairy goat in his house, except that it would sanctify that house and Blessings upon them. So if there were two, so there would be sanctity and Blessings upon them twice a day’. One of them said, ‘And how are they sanctified?’ He (a.s.) said: ‘An Angel pauses at them every morning, or evening, so he says to them: ‘May you be sanctified and Blessings be upon you, and may you be good and your food be good’. I said to him (a.s.), ‘And what is the meaning of ‘Sanctify you’?’ He (a.s.) said: ‘Purify you’.

And the one who had two sheep in his house, the Angels sanctify him twice, and do away the poverty from him with a removal. So if there were three sheep, the Angels sanctify him with three sanctifications, and they remove the poverty from him’.
