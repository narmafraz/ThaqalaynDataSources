13. Equipment of the animals

Back to book

- عنه، عن على بن أسباط، عن على بن جعفر، عن أخيه قال: سألت عن ركوب جلود السباع؟ - قال: لا بأس ما لم يسجد عليها.


1. From him, from Ali Bin Asbaat, (It has been narrated) from Ali son of Ja’far (a.s.), from his brother (a.s.) (7th Imam (a.s.)), said, ‘I asked him (a.s.) about the riding upon the wild animal skins?’ He (a.s.) said: ‘There is no problem what is not Prostrated upon’.

Hadith to report:

1. From him, from Ali Bin Asbaat, (It has been narrated) from Ali son of Ja’far (a.s.), from his brother (a.s.) (7th Imam (a.s.)), said, ‘I asked him (a.s.) about the riding upon the wild animal skins?’ He (a.s.) said: ‘There is no problem what is not Prostrated upon’.


عنه، عن عثمان، عن سماعة، قال: سئل أبو عبد الله (ع) من جلود السباع؟ فقال: اركبوا ولا تلبسوا شيئا منها تصلون فيه.


2. From him, from Usman, from Sama’at who said, ‘Abu Abdullah (a.s.) was asked about the skins of the wild animals?’ So he (a.s.) said: ‘Ride (upon it) and do not wear anything from these which you are Praying in it’.

3. From him, from Usman, from Sama’at who said, ‘I heard Abu Abdullah (a.s.) saying: ‘The Prophet (saww) said: ‘Beware of riding with a red saddlecloth, for it is a saddlecloth of Iblees (la)’.

Hadith to report:

3. From him, from Usman, from Sama’at who said, ‘I heard Abu Abdullah (a.s.) saying: ‘The Prophet (saww) said: ‘Beware of riding with a red saddlecloth, for it is a saddlecloth of Iblees (la)’.


عنه، عن أبيه، عن محمد بن على، عن عبد الرحمان بن أبى هاشم، عن إبراهيم بن يحيى المدينى، عن أبى عبد الله، أن على بن الحسين (ع) كان يركب على قطيفة حمراء.


4. From him, from his father, from Muhammad Bin Ali, from Abdul Rahman Bin Abu Hashim, from Ibrahim Bin Yahya Al Madainy, (It has been narrated) from Abu Abdullah (a.s.) that Ali (a.s.) Bin Al-Husayn (a.s.) rode upon red velvet’.
