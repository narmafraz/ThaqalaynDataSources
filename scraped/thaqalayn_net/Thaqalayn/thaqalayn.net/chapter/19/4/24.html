24. The Sweet Basil

Back to book

- عنه، عن على بن حسان، عمن حدثه، عن السكوني، عن أبى عبد الله (ع) قال: كأنى أنظر إلى نبات الباذروج في الجنة، قال: قلت له: الهندبا؟ - قال: لا، بل الباذروج.


1. From him, from Ali Bin Hasaan, from the one who narrated it, from Al Sakuny, (It has been narrated) from Abu Abdullah (a.s.) having said: ‘It is as if I (a.s.) am looking at the sweet basil growing in the Paradise’. I said to him (a.s.), ‘The endive?’ He (a.s.) said: ‘No, but the sweet basil’.

2. From him, form Muhammad Bin Ali, from Isa Bin Abdullah Al Alawy, from his father, from his grandfather, (It has been narrated) from Ali (a.s.) having said: ‘Rasool-Allah (saww) looked at the sweet basil, so he (saww) said: ‘This is the sweet basil, it is as if I (a.s.) am looking at it sprouting in the Paradise’.

عنه، عن النوفلي، عن السكوني، عن أبى عبد الله (ع) قال: قال على (ع) كان يعجب رسول الله صلى الله عليه وآله من البقول الحوك. قال: وسئل أبو عبد الله (ع) عن الحوك فقال: محبة إلى الناس غير أنها تبخر، والديدان تسرع إليها وهى الباذروج.


9. From him, from Al Nowfaly, (It has been narrated) from Abu Abdullah (a.s.) having said: ‘Ali (a.s.) said: ‘What Rasool-Allah (saww) admired from the vegetables, was the sweet basil’. He (the narrator) said: ‘And Abu Abdullah (a.s.) was asked about the sweet basil, so he (a.s.) said: ‘The people love it except that it dries up and the insects are quick to it, and it is the sweet basil’.
