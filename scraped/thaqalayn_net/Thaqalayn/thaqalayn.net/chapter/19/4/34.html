34. The Pumpkin

Back to book

- عنه، عن محمد بن عيسى، عن محمد بن عرفة، عن أبى الحسن الرضا (ع) قال: شجرة اليقطين هي الدباء وهو القرع.


1. From him, from Muhammad Bin Isa, from Muhammad Bin Arafat, Abu Al-Hassan Al-Ridha (a.s.) has said: ‘The tree of Al-Yaqteen it is the gourd, and it is the pumpkin’.

1. From him, from Muhammad Bin Isa, from Muhammad Bin Arafat, Abu Al-Hassan Al-Ridha (a.s.) has said: ‘The tree of Al-Yaqteen it is the gourd, and it is the pumpkin’.


عنه، عن النوفلي، عن السكوني، عن أبى عبد الله (ع) أن عليا (ع) سئل عن القرع، هل يذبح؟ - قال: القرع ليس شيئا يذكى، فكلوه ولا تذبحوه، ولا يستهوينكم الشيطان.


2. From him, from Al Nowfaly, from Al Sakuny, (It has been narrated) from Abu Abdullah (a.s.) that Ali (a.s.) was asked about the pumpkin, is it to be slaughtered?’ He (a.s.) said: ‘The pumpkin is not something you purify, therefore eat it and do not slaughter it, nor let the Satan (la) make you trivialise (Religion)’.
