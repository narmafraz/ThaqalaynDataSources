H 14620 – Ali, from his father and Muhammad Bin Ismail, from Al-Fazl Bin Shazaan altogether from Ibn Abu Umeyr, from Abd Al-Rahmaan Bin Al-Hajjaaj, and Hafs Bin Al-Bakhtary, and Salmat Bayya Al-Sabiry, who has narrated the following:

Abu Abdullahasws said: ‘Whenever Aliasws Bin Al-Husaynasws used to take the Book of Aliasws, heasws would look into it and say: ‘Who can endure this, who can endure this?’ Then heasws said: ‘Who would act upon it, and when heasws would stand for the Prayer, hisasws colour would change to the extent that it would become apparent in hisasws face, and no one can endure the actions of Aliasws from hisasws sonsasws from after himasws except for Aliasws Bin Al-Husaynasws’.
