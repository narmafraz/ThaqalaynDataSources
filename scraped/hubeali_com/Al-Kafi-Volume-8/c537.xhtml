H 14938 – A number of our companions, Ahmad Bin Muhammad Bin Khalid, from Ismail Bin Mahraan, from Sayf Bin Umeyra, from Basheer Al-Nabaal, from Humraan Bin Ayn who said:

I said to Abu Ja’farasws, ‘The people are saying the earth would fold up for us at night, how it would fold up?’ Heasws said: ‘Like this’, then folded hisasws cloth’.

14939- عَلِيُّ بْنُ إِبْرَاهِيمَ عَنْ أَبِيهِ عَنِ ابْنِ أَبِي عُمَيْرٍ عَنْ حَمَّادِ بْنِ عُثْمَانَ عَنْ أَبِي عَبْدِ اللَّهِ (عليه السلام) قَالَ الْأَرْضُ تُطْوَى فِي آخِرِ اللَّيْلِ.
