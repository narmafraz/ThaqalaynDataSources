H 14963 – Aban, from Abu Baseer, who has narrated the following:

Abu Abdullahasws having said: ‘Whenever the Rasool Allahsaww descended from hissaww she-camel Al-Qaswa, hesaww would leave its reins upon it. It would go out and come to the Muslims. So this man would give it something, and that man would give it something. So it would not remain without (its hunger) is being satisfied. It entered its head in a tent of Samarat Bin Jundab, so he took hold of a stick and struck it upon its head. So he scratched it. So it went out to the Prophetsaww and complained to himsaww’.

14964- أَبَانٌ عَنْ رَجُلٍ عَنْ أَبِي عَبْدِ اللَّهِ (عليه السلام) قَالَ إِنَّ مَرْيَمَ (عليها السلام) حَمَلَتْ بِعِيسَى (عليه السلام) تِسْعَ سَاعَاتٍ كُلُّ سَاعَةٍ شَهْراً.
